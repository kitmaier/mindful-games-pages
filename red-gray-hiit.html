The goal of this game is to recognize what color is showing and respond to it as quickly as possible without making too many mistakes. Tap the "A" key if you see red and the "D" key if you see gray, or use the Red and Gray buttons below. If you see the same color in a row, just keep hitting the same button until it changes. As you get comfortable, notice that your hand starts trying to predict the color ahead of time, leading to mistakes. The color cannot be predicted ahead of time, because it is completely random. Focus on recognizing and suppressing that reaction, and trying to just go with what you see in front of you. This game will go in intervals of 20 seconds off / 20 seconds on.
