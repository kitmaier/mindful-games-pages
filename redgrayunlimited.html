The goal of this game is to go as fast as possible without making too many mistakes. As you get comfortable, notice that your hand starts trying to predict the color ahead of time, leading to mistakes. The color cannot be predicted ahead of time, because it is completely random. Focus on recognizing and suppressing that reaction, and trying to just go with what you see in front of you. Scores will be a running calculation based on the last 20 events. Tap the "A" key if you see red and the "D" key if you see gray, or use the Red and Gray buttons below.

Tap either key to start.
